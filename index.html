A Renaissance Fantasy

The setting of A Renaisssance Fantasy is inspired by the historical early modern period, namely 16th century Europe. Here you can stay at cozy harbours, journey to lone mansions, visit gilded palaces and gothic cities.

Outside of habitable location, the depiction of landscapes with fantastical colour palettes emphasizes the sense of other-worldliness.
